Energy sources

There are many different sources of energy but they are all either renewable or nonrenewable energy sources. Renewable and nonrenewable energy sources can be used as primary energy sources to produce useful energy such as heat, or they can be used to produces secondary energy sources such as electricity and hydrogen.

  1. Renewable energy sources
    • Solar Power
      • Photovoltaic Solar Power
        • Residential solar panels
        • Commercial solar farms
      • Concentrated Wind Power
        • Solar thermal power plants
        • Solar water heating systems
    • Wind Energy
      • Onshore Wind Power
        • Wind farms on land
        • Small-scale wind turbines
      • Offshore Wind Power
        • Wind turbines installed in water
        • Floating wind farms

    2. Nonrenewable energy resources include coal, natural gas, and nuclear energy. Once these resources are used up, cannot be replaced, which is a major problem for humanity as we are currently dependent on them to supply most of our energy needs

  2. Non Renewable Energy Sources (Fossil Fuels)
    • Coal
    • Oil (Petroleum)
      • Crude Oil
        • Oil refineries
        • Petroleum products
      • Refined petroleum products
        • Gasoline distribution
        • Jet fuel production
    • Natural Gas
      • Conventional natural gas
        • Natural gas power plants
        • Residential and commercial heating
