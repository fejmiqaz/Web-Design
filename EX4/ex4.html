Attendance policy

Lectures and exercises are held every week, while laboratory exercises are scheduled every second week. Attendance is not just encouraged but mandatory. Attending lectures provides crucial insights into the course material, supplemented by interactive exercises that reinforce understanding. The bi-weekly laboratory exercises offer practical applications of theoretical knowledge, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for success in the field. Regular attendance ensures a comprehensive grasp of the curriculum and maximizes academic achievement. It also allows for meaningful interactions and peers, facilitating collaborative learning experiences and the exchange of ideas.

Improve studying skills

Knowing how to learn well is not something that happens overnight, but putting a few of these learning techniques into daily practice can help you get more out of your study time. There are a number of different strategies that can boost memory. Basic tips such as improving your focus. avoiding cram sessions, and structuring your study time are good places to start, but there are even more lessons from psychology that can dramatically improve your learning efficiency.
